About Us

Our horror themed park was founded in 2007 by John Horror who was bored with the routine design of theme parks. He had the vision of introducing a change to classic theme park designs. Since 2007 we are serving people of all ages looking for a thrill experience unlike any other.

Having a Safe Visit

Safety is our top priority in our park. Although we try to make sure to provide a safe experiment as much as possible we advise our visitors to follow some advice for a comfortable visit.

  • Do not leave your kids unattended except for designated playgrounds.
  • Keep your belongings with you all the time.
  • Make sure to see your doctor before your visit if you have any health issues.
